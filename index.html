Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DevConf 2026.

When and where is DevConf 2026 taking place?

DevConf 2026 will be held on September 18-20, 2026 at the San Francisco Convention Center, San Francisco, California.

Is the conference available online?

Yes! All keynotes and main stage talks will be live streamed. Pro and Team ticket holders also get lifetime access to all session recordings.

What is the refund policy?

Full refunds are available up to 30 days before the event. After that, tickets can be transferred to another person but are non-refundable.

Are meals included in the ticket price?

Standard and Pro tickets include networking lunch and coffee breaks on all three days. Pro ticket holders also get access to the VIP networking dinner.

Can I switch my ticket type after purchasing?

Yes, you can upgrade your ticket at any time before the event by paying the difference. Downgrades are not permitted after purchase.

Is there a discount for students or groups?

Yes! Students get a 40% discount with a valid student ID. For groups of 5 or more, please contact us directly for special pricing.
